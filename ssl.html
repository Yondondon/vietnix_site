The Internet world is becoming more and more dangerous

Internet users are always cautious and only trust access to the Website with SSL security certificates. To protect your website and prevent information theft, use SSL today!

Benefits of SSL registration

Secure authentication

SSL registered website will display protocol https blue, Secure text or lock image. This is a sign for Internet users to easily recognize and trust website access.

Information security encryption

All data transferred back and forth on the website are encrypted and secure. This is extremely important to avoid theft of personal or payment account information.

Good for Google SEO

SSL certificate has become one of the important criteria for Google to assess the credibility and authenticity of the website. SSL registration is almost mandatory for SEO.

Increase brand identity

SSL Enterprise uses EV authentication method, on the Address Bar of the browser will display the prominent Blue Business Name, helping to increase brand recognition and reputation.
