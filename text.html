1. Customer Information Vietnix is ​​allowed to store

For individual customers

Personal information includes: Full name, address, gender, identity card number, ...

Contact information includes: email, phone number, social network channels, permanent address, ...

System information includes: user account and IP address to access the service at Vietnix.

For corporate customers

Subject information includes: business information, legal representation, service contracts, ...

Contact information includes: legal representatives, phone numbers, emails, operating addresses, ...

System information includes: user account and IP address to access the service at Vietnix.

2. Purpose to Store Customer Information

Serving customer management and support

Ensure adequate legal documents for services before the law

Contact the customer when there are changes to notify

Product and brand advertising

3. Store Customer Information

Customer information will be stored in writing at the company office and stored on the customer management system installed on Vietnix-owned servers.

4. Time to Store Customer Information

All customer information will be retained during the time when the customer is using the service and 12 months after the end of the service.

5. Share Customer Information

Being allowed to share information with cooperating companies to develop market or develop products for Vietnix.

It is allowed to provide customer information to authorized law enforcement agencies, or in cases where we need to provide evidence to protect legitimate rights of Vietnix.

6. Commitment to Secure Customer Information

Vietnix commits that all customer information is kept confidential in many best forms.

We are committed to not selling or renting or sharing with units not mentioned in section 5.

All customer information is securely encrypted into digital data stored safely on Vietnix's server.
